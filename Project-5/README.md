## ðŸ“ Conclusion

This study clearly demonstrates that life expectancy is a multidimensional outcome, influenced by health infrastructure, economic stability, education, and disease prevention.

- Increasing healthcare investment

- Strengthening immunization programs
- Raising educational standards
- Reducing HIV prevalence & malnutrition
- Improving lifestyle awareness programs
